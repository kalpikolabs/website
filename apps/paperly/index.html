Paperly

PDF & image tools that never leave your phone

Coming soon

Email me when it launches →

Paperly is a private, on-device PDF & image toolkit for anyone who needs to quickly fix a document or photo on their phone. Version 1.0 covers the four highest-demand jobs: merge PDFs, compress a PDF to fit upload limits, turn photos into a single PDF, and convert images between HEIC, JPG and PNG.

Every operation runs 100% on your device — no account, no uploads, no ads, no tracking, and no analytics SDKs. It’s free to try with 3 operations (lifetime, across all tools), and unlimited via $2.99/mo, $11.99/yr, or a $19.99 one-time lifetime unlock. The v1.1 roadmap adds split, reorder/rotate/delete pages, PDF→images, lock/unlock, watermark, image resize/compress, and on-device OCR — all keeping the same on-device promise.

A look inside

Good to know

Do my files get uploaded?

No. Every operation runs on your device; nothing is ever uploaded, and there’s no account.

Is there a free version?

Yes — 3 operations free (lifetime, across all tools). Unlimited is a one-time $19.99, or $2.99/mo / $11.99/yr.

Can Paperly unlock a PDF I don’t have the password for?

No. Unlock requires the password you already know; Paperly never cracks protection.

A note on scope: v1.0 ships 4 tools (merge, compress, photos→PDF, image convert); split, rotate, lock/unlock, watermark, resize, and OCR arrive in v1.1. There’s no PDF→editable Word/Excel/PPT and no AI features — both would require a server, which would break the on-device promise.
