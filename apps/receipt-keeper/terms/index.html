You can cancel anytime from your App Store or Google Play account settings; cancellation stops the next renewal and takes effect at the end of the current period. Scope & accuracy

Receipt Keeper is a tool to capture, organize, and search your own receipts. It does not connect to your bank, does not access any financial account, processes no payments, and gives no financial advice.

AI extraction accuracy depends on photo clarity, and unreadable receipts can be edited manually. Warranty periods are estimated (roughly 12 months for electronics, 24 months for appliances and furniture) and should be verified against the actual product warranty. Receipt Keeper’s reminders are a convenience, not a guarantee.
