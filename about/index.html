About

The Lab

from kalpana (कल्पना) — to imagine, to form, to invent.

Why “Kalpiko”?

The root is the Sanskrit kalpana — the act of imagining, forming, inventing. It’s the moment before a thing exists, when it’s still just an idea worth building well.

“Kalpiko” itself is a coined word — an invented name for a studio whose whole job is to invent. It doesn’t mean anything yet. That’s the point: it means whatever we make of it.

How we build

Small, sharp, honest.

Small

Apps that do one thing genuinely well, instead of ten things badly.

Sharp

A native feel on each platform — fast, quiet, and free of bloat.

Honest

Clear pricing, plain-English privacy, and no dark patterns. Ever.

Who’s behind this

Kalpiko Labs is an independent developer building a small lineup of apps in public — shipping early, writing about the process, and listening to the people who actually use the things.

It’s a studio of one, on purpose. Fewer apps, more care, and a real person on the other end of every email.
